Red Cross Intel

An intelligence briefing platform for American Red Cross leadership. Powered by a knowledge graph of 111,000+ entities covering every US county.

Getting Started

  • National shows the full US overview with division comparisons
  • Click Division, Region, or Chapter tabs to drill down
  • Click any county on the map to see its detail card

Tabs

  • Overview — KPI summary + executive briefing
  • Counties — Map metrics, search counties, top rankings
  • Ask — Ask natural language questions about your area (powered by SQL + Knowledge Graph)

Data Sources

FEMA National Risk Index, CDC Social Vulnerability Index, United Way ALICE, FEMA disaster declarations, Red Cross FLARE fire incidents (CY2024), and Wikipedia county profiles.
